# Sprint Retrospective

## What went well

1. The team strictly followed the Definition of Done, resulting in zero merged PRs without approval. All code reviews were completed, and every PR had at least one approval before merging.

2. Communication in the Telegram group was effective for quick blocker resolution. Team members responded within minutes, which helped maintain sprint velocity.

3. The new issue templates (User Story, PBI, Course Task, Bug Report) saved significant time during backlog creation and ensured consistency across all tasks.

## What did not go well

1. Telegram Bot testing was challenging because Telegram is blocked in Russia. Team members had to use VPN, which slowed down development and testing of authentication flows.

2. New requirements from the customer appeared during the sprint (e.g., daily notifications, statistics feature), which caused scope creep and required re-prioritization of the backlog.

3. Organizing synchronous meetings with the team was difficult due to different student schedules. This led to delays in decision-making and required more asynchronous communication.

2. Update the root README.md with a detailed, step-by-step local environment setup guide, including VPN instructions for Telegram Bot testing. This will help new team members onboard faster and reduce setup time. (Owner: @veronika1977)
